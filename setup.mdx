### Installing the plugin to the Roblox Studio

Once you have the rbxm file downloaded on your PC, you can go to: **File Explorer** \> **This PC** \> **Local Disk** \> **Users** \> **User** \> **AppData** \> **Local** \> **Roblox** \> **Plugins**

Like these:

![Screenshot2025 08 09092708 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-09092708.png)

Or just open the roblox studio and edit any of your existing roblox place/create a new one, and head to the plugins tab and click plugins folder, then insert the downloaded rbxm file into the plugins folder.

![Screenshot2025 08 09093011 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-09093011.png)

![Screenshot2025 08 09093256 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-09093256.png)

![Screenshot2025 08 09093412 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-08-09093412.png)
